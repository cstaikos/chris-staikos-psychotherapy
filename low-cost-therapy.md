  <h1>Low-cost online therapy</h1>
  <p class="lead">I offer a limited number of reduced-fee therapy spots for individuals and couples who are looking for a more affordable way to access care.</p>
</section>

<section class="section-grid">
  <article class="card">
    <h2>Reduced Fees</h2>
    <p>Low-cost therapy is offered at $65 for individuals and $85 for couples. These fees are significantly reduced from my regular session rates of $150 for individuals and $170 for couples.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="card">
    <h2>Short-Term Model</h2>
    <p>To help make low-cost therapy available to more people, sessions at this rate are offered within a short-term model of 6 sessions. Sessions are 60 minutes and take place remotely via Zoom.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="card">
    <h2>Planning the Next Step</h2>
    <p>After the sixth session, there may be an option to continue in a sliding scale spot, if one is available, or at the full fee. If neither option is feasible, our therapeutic relationship will come to a close after the sixth session.</p>
    <p>A free 20-minute consultation is a good place to talk through fit, availability, and whether a low-cost spot is currently open.</p>
