  <p class="eyebrow">Breathwork</p>
  <h1>Breath-based practices to support regulation, awareness, and deeper connection.</h1>
  <p class="lead">Breathwork can complement psychotherapy by helping clients reconnect with their bodies, shift patterns of activation, and access a fuller sense of presence.</p>
</section>

<section class="section-grid section-grid-two">
  <article class="card">
    <h2>Why breathwork</h2>
    <p>It offers an embodied pathway into healing, especially when insight alone does not fully shift what is being felt in the nervous system.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="card">
    <h2>How it is offered</h2>
    <p>Gently, collaboratively, and with attention to safety, pacing, and integration.</p>
  </article>
